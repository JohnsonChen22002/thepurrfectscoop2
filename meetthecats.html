Marvin

Marvin is a jumpy cat who enjoys napping in small boxes and being pet but never picked up. He has wonderful paw-eye coordination which he uses to have fun with his many toys. Marvin enjoys making direct eye contact, head-butting people in their legs, and eating treats.
