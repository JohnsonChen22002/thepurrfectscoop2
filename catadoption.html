Cat Adoption

At the Purrfect Scoop, we are committed to promoting the importance of cat adoption and providing resources for visitors to learn more.

Main Reasons why to adopt a cat!

  • You will save a life
  • It will cost you less
  • Your home will provide a better place for your cat

    • You'll get a great animal.
    • You'll get bragging rights.
    • All pets are good for your health, but adoptees offer an extra boost.
    • The Shelter Pet Project makes it easy.

Misconceptions about Cat Adoption

  • "Shelter pets have to much baggage"

    • Rescued pets have full histories - something that can actually be GREAT for adopters.

  • "I cant find what I want at a shelter"

    • At Petfinder.com, they can e-mail you when a pet that fits your criteria is posted.

  • "I can get a free pet, so why pay an adoption fee"

    • According to the National Council on Pet Population Study and Policy (via the ASPCA), approximately 65% of pet parents in the U.S. get their pets for free or at low cost, and most pets are obtained from acquaintances or family members.
